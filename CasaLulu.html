Village Rules

Welcome to our vibrant and close-knit community! To ensure a harmonious and thriving environment, we have established the following rules and guidelines for all residents and visitors of our village:

1. Respect for Others:

- Treat all residents, visitors, and their property with kindness and respect.

- Avoid disruptive or offensive behavior that may disturb the peace of the community.

2. Noise Control:

- Keep noise levels down between 10:00 PM and 7:00 AM to ensure a peaceful night's rest for all.

- Use headphones when listening to music or watching videos in shared spaces.

3. Property Maintenance:

- Maintain your property in good condition to uphold the aesthetic appeal of the village.

- Follow local regulations regarding property upkeep, landscaping, and trash disposal.

4. Pets:

- Ensure that your pets are well-behaved and properly cared for.

- Clean up after your pets when walking them in common areas.

- Comply with leash and vaccination requirements.

5. Safety and Security:

- Report any suspicious activity to local law enforcement.

- Keep doors and windows locked when not at home.

- Follow safety guidelines for fire prevention and emergency procedures.

These rules are designed to maintain a safe, pleasant, and inclusive community for everyone. By abiding by these guidelines, we can continue to enjoy the beauty and camaraderie of our village. Thank you for your cooperation and commitment to making our village a wonderful place to live.
